**Plastics**

* Modular Roof Rack System – Various 3d printed pieces custom designed on Fusion 360 to attach gear to the roof rack of a 2000 Jeep Cherokee.
* Wheel Center Caps – 3d printed and designed to fit the wheels of my family's cars to protect the wheel hub
* Misc. Car parts – Various small 3d printed supports, clips, and battery terminal covers to replace parts on my car that are difficult to find and replace.
* Planetary gears – A school project where I designed gears and machined them with a CNC mill and built a case out of laser cut acrylic.



**Metal**

* Removable Winch Plate – A mount, designed, cut, and welded to fit in a car's hitch receiver and carry a winch for off-roading self rescue.
* C-clamp – A school project put together using a screw and pad, both made on a lathe, and a frame made with a bandsaw and a mill.
* Tire Changer Upgrades – Upgraded and reinforced a harbor freight tire changer.
* Metal Die and Stand – An aluminum block milled into the shape of a die, and a sand casted display stand.
* Lathe tool – Used a grinding wheel to make a lathe tool out of a small blank of tool steel.

**Wood**

* Chicken coop - Designed and built a chicken coop out of spare wood in my parent's yard.
