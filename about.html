About Me

Based in Southern California, Emily Caldwell is a mechanical engineer with a passion for designing, building, and improving things both professionally and in her personal projects. She enjoys creating things, solving problems, and learning new skills through hands-on projects.

Outside of engineering, she has a strong interest in military aircraft and high-performance cars, appreciating the innovation and engineering behind them. Whether working with metal, plastics, wood, or digital design tools, she enjoys creating practical solutions and continually expanding her skills through new challenges and projects.
